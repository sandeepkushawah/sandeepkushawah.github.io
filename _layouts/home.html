Research focus

Why does a cooling liquid get stuck?

When a liquid is cooled fast enough to dodge crystallization, its dynamics slow by more than ten orders of magnitude while its structure barely changes — the glass transition. I build large-scale molecular dynamics simulations of model binary mixtures and develop new structural and entropy-based order parameters to explain where that hidden order lives and how it drives slow, spatially heterogeneous relaxation.
