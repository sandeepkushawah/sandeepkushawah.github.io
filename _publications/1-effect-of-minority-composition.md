**Abstract.** This study investigates the effect of minority composition in a binary glass-forming liquid system. It explores the structural and dynamic properties that govern the glass-transition behavior at varying compositions, providing insight into the molecular dynamics that contribute to the glass-forming ability of different systems.

**Keywords:** binary glass, minority composition, glass transition, molecular dynamics, structural properties, dynamic properties.
